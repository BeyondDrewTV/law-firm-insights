### ReviewRail — Last verified: Jan 2026
| Plan | Price | Included | Notes |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| Starter | $79/mo | 1 location, 500 reviews/mo, monthly PDF report | Annual = $69/mo |
| Growth | $149/mo | 3 locations, unlimited reviews, dashboard access | Most popular |
| Agency | $349/mo | 10 locations, white-label PDF, API access | Targets MSPs |

**Observations:** No per-seat pricing. Annual discount ~13%. No free trial — 14-day money-back. No benchmarking or competitive data included at any tier.

---

### Lexical Insights — Last verified: Feb 2026
| Plan | Price | Included | Notes |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| Professional | ~$800/mo | Up to 50K records/mo, 3 users, standard NLP | Requires 12-mo contract |
| Enterprise | Custom (~$2,500+/mo) | Unlimited records, custom models, dedicated CSM | Requires professional services onboarding ($5K–$15K) |

**Observations:** High ACV ($10K–$30K+), long sales cycle (60–90 days), not self-serve. Targets legal ops at large firms. Not a direct SMB competitor but could expand down-market.

## Clarion Positioning Summary (vs. Competitors)
- Clarion's entry price (~$199/mo planned) is above ReviewRail Starter but below ReviewRail Growth at comparable feature depth
- Clarion's scoring model and benchmarking have no direct equivalent in any tracked competitor
- Primary win condition vs. ReviewRail: dashboard + benchmarking + CS workflow
- Primary win condition vs. FirmSignal: analytics depth, scoring, actionable output
- Primary loss risk vs. Lexical: enterprise buyers who want custom NLP and have budget
