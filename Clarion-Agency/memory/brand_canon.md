# Clarion — Brand Voice & Positioning
# Version: 1.0 | All agents must follow this when producing any external-facing or marketing content.

---

## Positioning

Clarion is a **governance and operational insight platform** for law firms. It helps firm leaders understand what clients are actually saying — and turn that signal into decisions that improve how the firm runs.

---

## Target Customer

Mid-to-large law firms where managing partners and operations leaders are accountable for client experience, reputation, and internal governance.

---

## Product Category

Client feedback intelligence for legal governance. Not a review aggregator. Not a reputation management tool. A structured insight system that connects client voice to firm leadership.

---

## Tone

Professional. Precise. Understated. Clarion earns trust through clarity, not enthusiasm.

- Write like a trusted advisor, not a vendor.
- Prefer plain language over legal or technical jargon.
- Let the insight speak — avoid editorializing.

---

## Messaging Principles

- Lead with outcomes, not features.
- Speak to the decision-maker, not the evaluator.
- Ground every claim in what the product actually does.
- Specificity outperforms superlatives.

---

## Claims We Avoid

- "AI-powered" or "AI-driven" in any client-facing context — scoring is deterministic, not AI.
- Superlatives: best, leading, most powerful, revolutionary.
- Anything resembling legal advice or legal outcome guarantees.
- Implying that Clarion replaces human judgment.
