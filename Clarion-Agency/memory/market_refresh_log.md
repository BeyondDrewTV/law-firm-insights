# Clarion Agency — Market Freshness Log

> **Rule:** Market-facing agents must refresh external market understanding at least every 4 weeks.
> **Affected agents:** Competitive Intelligence · Customer Discovery · Head of Growth · Content & SEO
>
> Each entry must be appended by the logging agent. Never overwrite a prior entry.
> Chief of Staff checks this log every run. If the most recent entry is older than 30 days,
> Chief of Staff flags the stale division and instructs it to refresh market intelligence.

---

## Log Entry Format

```
---
DATE:               [YYYY-MM-DD]
AGENT:              [Competitive Intelligence | Customer Discovery | Head of Growth | Content & SEO]
MARKET SIGNALS:     [Summary of new market signals discovered — or None.]
COMPETITOR CHANGES: [Competitor moves, feature releases, pricing changes, messaging shifts — or None.]
INDUSTRY NEWS:      [Relevant legal tech or law firm industry news — or None.]
PRICING CHANGES:    [Any observed pricing moves in the market — or None.]
NOTES:              [Optional context — e.g. thin data week, no new signals found]
---
```
